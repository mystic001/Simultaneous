Simultaneous equation

a set of two or more equations, each containing two or more variables whose values can simultaneously satisfy both or all the equations in the set, the number of variables being equal to or less than the number of equations in the set..

The right side of this page is a workspace for you to enter the values of your equation and get the corresponding value of your unknown

Please take note that it is already assumed here that your unknown is x and y ..incase you have a question that states the unknown to be other letters apart from x and y, never mind just enter the coeffient of the letter that you have in your equation
